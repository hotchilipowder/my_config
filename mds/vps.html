Local Machine¶

对于Ubuntu, 设置网络的方式和Debian有所区别。

network:
    ethernets:
        enp10s0:
            dhcp4: true
            addresses:
            - 192.168.123.x1/24
            nameservers:
                addresses:
                - 1.1.1.1
                - 192.168.123.x2
                search: []
            routes:
            -   to: default
                via: 192.168.123.x2
    version: 2
    wifis: {}
